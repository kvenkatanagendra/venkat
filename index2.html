tea

It is one of the most popular hot beverages in world and especially in India

Ingredients:

2 cups Milk
2 teaspoons Tea Powder (chai patti)
3 teaspoons Sugar

Preparation

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add sugar and tea powder in it and boil it for 3-4 minutes on medium flame.
  3. Add milk and boil it over medium flame for 6-7 minutes or until bubble starts to rise.
  4. You will see the change in color of the tea from milky shade to brown shade when it is ready
  5. Turn off the gas and strain tea in cups.

    6. Tips and Variations:

      If you like more creamy tea, then avoid water and use only milk. Mix milk, sugar and tea powder and boil them over low flame for 6-7 minutes.
      If you do not like it to creamy, then use more water compared with milk.
      Add ginger, mint, cinnamon and cardamom or tea masala while preparing it for nice flavorful tea
